Financial Status
Overview

The Financial Status field is used as part of the Vendor Details screen.

It's purpose is to tag how a Vendor performs or is rated financially.

This can typically take the form of a RAG status (Red, Amber, Green). The Financial Status of the Vendor may dictate the course of future business with that Vendor.

Required Information

The definition of a Financial Status is a single textual definition.

Enter the text definition that you wish the status to be known by and click the 'Update' button.

On successful update, you will be returned to the definitions list screen.
