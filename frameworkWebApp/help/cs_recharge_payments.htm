Recharge Payments
Overview

In order for recharges to be applied for given products, the payments must be generated.

Once these recharge payments have been generated, the recharge costs can be reported upon or committed as a forecasted invoice entry.

By default, recharge payments are displayed for the current quarter. The current quarter is assumed to be a month either side of the current month.

The dates for which the recharge payments are displayed can be changed easily. Simply modify the date range at the top of the screen to the date range you wish to display and click the refresh button.

Note: Any payments generated are calculated according to the recharge template for the given product and recharge party. You should ensure that these specifications are correct prior to creating charges, otherwise manual modification of the items will be necessary.
Generating the Recharge Payments

To create recharge payments for a specified period, click the 'Generate' button.

You will be prompted to specify the dates for which you want to create the payments for.

Enter the date range required and click 'OK'.

Recharge payments for the defined period are now created at the frequency specified in the global recharge frequency parameter. This may be specified as monthly, quarterly etc.
