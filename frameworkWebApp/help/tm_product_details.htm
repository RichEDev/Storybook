Product Details
Overview
Contracts, on the whole, have some tangible element that is serviced or delivered by the contract agreement. These are generically known within the Framework application as Products.

Framework is designed to manage the individual deliverable items covered by the contract and these can be maintained via the Contract Products section of the Contract Summary pages.

Product Details is the administration area of the application where the individual items can be defined for use within the application.

To add a new Product, simply click the Add link in the page options panel from the product details screen. A blank product page will be displayed.
Locate an existing Product
On accessing the Product Details page, the filter options are displayed on the screen.

Product Filter allows a wildcard value to be typed in the box supplied. Clicking the icon will search and return all products that contain the search sting anywhere in its name.

Update or Delete a Product
If the product being sought appears in the list, simply click the edit icon () to the left of the product and it will be displayed in detail for modification.
