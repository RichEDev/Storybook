Sales Tax
Overview

Although relatively simplistic in the UK, other nations have different taxations that have to be paid dependent on contractual area, geographical region etc.

The Sales Tax definition is supplied against contract products to identify what, if any, tax is included in the monetary values specified. This has no direct calculation or effect within the application and is there purely for reference.

Required Information

The primary definition is a text definition that must be unique. Any duplicate defintions provided will be rejected.

Also provided is the percentage that the tax specified equates to. Again, this is purely for reference and at present has no direct calculable effect on the monetary values specified.
