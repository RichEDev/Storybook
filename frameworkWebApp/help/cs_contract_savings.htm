Contract Savings
Overview
Contract Savings is provided as a means of logging when, whether by alternative sourcing or by negotiation, a product or service has been obtained at a better rate than previously.

The saving made from the annual budget is often interpreted as being as good a 10 times the equivalent made in profit.

To this end, many people in the procurement, finance and contract roles have their performance judged by the amount of savings they are able to make annually from previous years.

Such savings can be logged and associated with the contract in question in this area of the application, thus allowing accurate reporting of savings made over a given period of time and what areas of the business.
View current savings
On entering the contract savings screen, the display dates for savings is defaulted to display only savings made during the previous three months to the current date.

Simple modify the date range at the top of the screen and click the refresh ( ) button to display the desired saving entries.
Adding a saving entry
To add a saving, simply click on the Add Saving link in the page options panel.

The savings fields are displayed. Fill in the relevant details of the saving that has been made and click the Update button. The saving is then added to the system and should be displayed in the list (assuming it falls within the current date range specified).

To abandon the adding of a saving, simply click the Cancel button at any time.
Note: The ability to add a saving is only available if the user's role permits it.
Amending or Deleting a saving entry
In order to amend or delete a saving entry, simply click the icon in the two left columns of the table.

Clicking the Edit icon () will display the fields. Once the desired modifications have been made, click the update button to commit them to the database. Click the cancel button at any time to abandon any changes being made.

Click the delete icon() will prompt the user to confirm that they wish to remove the saving entry. Click OK on the dialog box. Doing so will permanently remove the saving entry from the database. Click CANCEL to abandon the deletion.
Note: The ability to edit or delete a saving is only available if the user's role permits it.
