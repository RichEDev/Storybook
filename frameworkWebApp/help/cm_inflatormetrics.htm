Inflator Metrics
Overview

Inflator Metrics are the economic indices used to measure acceptable inflation in different business areas.

The most common examples of Inflator Metrics are RPI, CEL or RPIX. These are typically published indices by Governmental bodies such as The Office of National Statistics.
