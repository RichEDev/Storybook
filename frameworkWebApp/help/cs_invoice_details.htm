Invoice Details
Overview

The Invoice Details section allows the storing on any invoices issued or received that relate to the current contract to be stored.

By storing any invoice information relating to the contract, not only is reporting capabilities enhanced, but it is possible to retain a history of what the costs of the contract are over a prolonged period (e.g. cost increases over consecutive terms of the contract).

TIP: By Default, only active invoices are displayed. Use Invoice Status drop-down list to display archived or all.

NOTE: The functionality provided in the invoice details section does not aim to replace any financial systems, merely enables the storage of particular contract related invoices.

Key Fields

Field Name Definition
Invoice Status

The status of the invoice is selected from a drop-down list and the options are defined by your organisation.

Each invoice status definition can have either a 'live' or 'archived' state. By default, only the 'live' invoices are displayed.

TIP: If you don't select a status, it will be not be displayed as 'live'. It will only display when 'Both' is selected from the status filter drop-down list.

Invoice Breakdown

Once a invoice has been added, it is possible to detail how the invoice amount is broken down. It may be that the invoice incorporates more than one contract product.

By clicking the 'breakdown' link at the bottom of the screen, you are transferred to the invoice breakdown section where the proportions of the invoice can be detailed.

NOTE: On returning from the breakdown screen, the invoice amount is automatically updated with the sum total of the breakdown elements.

Invoice Status History

The status of the invoice can be used to trace its lifecycle from receipt/issue to payment. For instance, an invoice may have to follow a specific sign-off procedure, prior to payment. Changing the status of the invoice would enable this to be logged.

The Invoice Status link transfers you to a screen that displays a history of these status transitions. The status history is read only and cannot be modified.

On deletion of an invoice, all status history will also be deleted for the deleted invoice.
