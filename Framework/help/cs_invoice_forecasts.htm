Invoice Forecast Details
Overview

The Forecast tab provides the capability to store known payments (or expected invoices) for a given contract.

These can either be entered individully or generated for the whole term of the contract automatically.

HINT: An automated email notification is available that will notify of overdue invoices. The forecasts specified in this section can be used to detect such expected payments that have not been invoiced. This is particularly important for pro-active management of budgets.
Forecast Functionality

Function Description
Move

For each invoice forecast added to the system, an additional action call 'Move' also appears to the left of the forecast detail. The action that this will result in is that the entry will be transferred from being a forecast to an actual invoice.

On clicking the 'Move' button, you will be transferred to the invoices tab of the contract summary and the details from the forecast will be automatically populated into the invoice. The remaining fields of the invoice can be filled in and the 'Update' button clicked.

What happens on updating the invoice depends on how the system's 'Keep Forecasts' option has been configured :-

If 'Keep Forecasts' = Yes then the invoice will be added to the system and the forecast entry will remain untouched in the forecasts screen.
OR
If 'Keep Forecasts' = No then the forecast entry will be removed on successfull entry of the invoice.

The forecast will have to be kept if the Variance or Budget reports are to be used from the Standard Reports.
Generate

The generate function allow quick creation of all required forecasts for a given contract period.

On selecting the option, the numbmer of forecasts dictated by the invoice frequency and duration of the contract are created.

The date range required for can be reduced or extended freely. Clicking the OK button will add the forecasts to the system.

NOTE: An equal breakdown of costs between the contract products will also be generated along with the individual forecast items.
Breakdown

Once a forecast has been entered, a product / amount breakdown button is enabled. This will enable the amount for the period to be divided into portions between the products assigned to the contract.

NOTE: On returning from the breakdown screen, the forecast amount is automatically updated with the sum total of the breakdown elements.
