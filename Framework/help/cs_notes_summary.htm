Notes Summary
Overview

Within Framework free text notes can be stored for the following areas of the database:-

  • Contract Notes

  • Product Notes

  • Invoice Notes

  • Vendor Notes

  • Vendor Contact Notes

    • The purpose of the Notes Summary page is to pool the Notes from each of the designated areas into one place where they can be reviewed.

    Only the notes that are displayed are those in context with the active contract.

    At times when the contract is being reviewed it is important to evaluate all related notes stored against the contract. By displaying together in the notes summary screen, the user is saved from having to move between multiple screens to find if any notes exist that are relevant to the active contract.

    TIP: Clicking the 'Open' button to the left of a note will open a fresh window where the whole note can be read more easily. The window is read-only and no editing can take place in this screen.
