Recharge Template
Overview

The recharge template screen is the key section where exactly how an individual products' costs is to be divided up and recharged between the other parties.

For each contract product, the individual parties who are to be recharged a proportion of the product cost, can be added to the template. The parties recharged for a proportion of the costs may differ between individual products, so by enabling the parties to be defined per product, this flexibility is provided.

Once the recargable parties have been added, how the recharge costs are proportioned can be specified.

The ability to specify a percentage surcharge per recharge line is also provided.

The actual generation of recharge payment entries within Framework, will be created according to the recharge template specification defined here.

Proportioning of Costs

The recharge proportion can be defined in three ways on a per party / product basis.

These are: -

Proportion Type Description
Percentage (%) A percentage of the cost of the product on the active contract will be calculated depending on the Current Maintenance cost of the contract.
Therefore, any change in the annual cost of the product will be reflected in the amount recharged.
Fixed Amount A static amount of money can be specified in this box. Regardless of any change in contractual cost of the product, the recharge amount will remain the same.
n Units If the product can be measured in a number of unit (e.g. Metres, Users, Sites, MIPS etc.), then the recharge can be specified as a number of these units.
The recharge will then automatically calculate what proportion of the total number of units for the product that this represents and calculate the recharge amount accordingly.
