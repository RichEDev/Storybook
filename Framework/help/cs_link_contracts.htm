Linked Contracts
Overview

The Linked Contracts tab of the Contract Summary screen is only displayed if the current contract is part of a group of contracts linked together by a common definition.

For every link definition that this contract is associated with, the definition and all it's linked contracts are displayed. The current contract is highlighted within each link definition group by the symbol.

To transfer to any of the other linked contracts, simply click the underlined hyperlink descriptions.

Note: Linked Contracts should not be confused with Schedule Links. Contracts on this screen may or may not be related. The only link between them is the definition under which they are tagged.
