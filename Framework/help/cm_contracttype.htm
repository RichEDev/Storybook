Contract Type
Overview

Contract Type definitions are the differing types of contract that need to be specified.

Typical examples of contract types are:

  • Maintenance Agreement
  • Licence Agreement
  • Service Contract

    • Required Information

    The definition of a contract type is a single textual definition.

    Enter the text definition that you wish the contract type to be known by and click the 'Update' button.

    On successful update, you will be returned to the definitions list screen.
