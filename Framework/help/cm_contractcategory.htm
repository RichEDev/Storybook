Contract Category
Overview

Contract Categories are one of the core definitions that allow contract records to be divided into their business or logical areas.

Categorizing contracts is, in effect, like filing your information in an orderly manner as opposed to just a mass bundle of information.

Choosing categories carefully can provide an invaluable criteria property when producting reports.

Note: Contract Categories are also used to allow the selective display of user defined field collections.

Required Information

The definition of a contract category is a single textual definition.

Enter the text definition that you wish the category to be known by and click the 'Update' button.

On successful update, you will be returned to the definitions list screen.
