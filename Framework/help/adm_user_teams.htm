User Teams
Overview
The definition of teams is provided to allow selection of a group of individuals (users or employees) to be collated under a common title. Once defined, teams can be selected for email notifications, ownership or access permission (to contracts or attachments).

The benefit of teams is that should an individual leave their post or move to a different role within the organisation, they only have to be removed from the team and not from every element within the database to which they have been assigned.

User Teams are groups of defined users (those with access to Framework via a logon user id).

A typical team definition may be to collate those users who work for a particular department of the organisation (e.g. Finance, Contract Management).

Where are they used?

User Teams are used to restrict access via the contract audience functionality or via an audience attachment.

In both these cases, once an audience has been assigned, only the distinct individuals or members of the team assigned may access the contract or attachment in question.

Once the required taggings have been completed, simply click the Update button to commit selections to the database. Click the Cancel button at any time to abandon action.
