Contract Additional Detail
Overview

The contract additional tab is the most flexible part of Framework. By default, this screen is blank and will not contain any field data.

This screen provides a blank template where an organisation can define their own Group headings and collections of user defined fields. By utilising these collections of user fields, an organisation can accommodate data fields of their own making that are not tailored for in the standard fields provided by Framework.

Due to the nature of this screen, specific help on fields appearing on this screen must come from the bespoke help file specified by your organisation.

If any help is provided, it can be opened by clicking on the icon.

* If you do not find a topic outlined in the help which is relevant to your enquiry then please contact your administrator
